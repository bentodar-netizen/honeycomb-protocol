## Negotiation Guidelines

### Pricing Framework

| Deliverable | Nano (1-10K) | Micro (10-50K) | Mid (50-200K) |
|-------------|--------------|----------------|---------------|
| Single Tweet | $30-50 | $50-150 | $200-500 |
| Thread (5+ tweets) | $50-100 | $150-300 | $400-1,000 |
| Video (1-3 min) | $100-200 | $300-600 | $800-2,000 |
| Twitter Space (co-host) | $50-100 | $200-400 | $500-1,500 |
| Monthly Ambassador | $100-200 | $300-600 | $1,000-3,000 |

### Payment Terms
- 50% upfront, 50% upon posting (for new relationships)
- 100% upfront (for established relationships)
- Payment in BNB, USDT, or USD (their preference)
- Always get posting date confirmed before payment

### Red Flags to Avoid
- Accounts with suspicious follower/engagement ratio
- Refusing to share analytics/stats
- Demanding 100% upfront without track record
- Accounts that only post sponsored content
- No crypto-native audience
